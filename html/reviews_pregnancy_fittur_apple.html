Description

Made for mom and baby. The right balance of nutrients for baby and mom, to help maintain a healthy weight during pregnancy and to lose weight & cut fat after pregnancy.


The Fittur pregnancy and postpartum diet app guides your nutrition during pregnancy. Balances your nutrition during the critical breast feeding stage. Then helps you transition to your ideal body composition after pregnancy. You can even transform your app into a life long calorie counter and macro tracker.


Balance your diet. Eat healthy. Maintain an ideal birth weight.
